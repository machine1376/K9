Our Proven Training Process

1

Foundation Building

We start with positive reinforcement in a low-distraction environment. Your dog learns basic commands while building trust and respect with the trainer.

2

Adding Structure

As your dog understands commands, we introduce appropriate corrections using leash and collar techniques. This creates clarity and reinforces expectations.

3

Real-World Distraction Training

Your dog practices commands around barking dogs, unfamiliar people, other animals, vehicles, and challenging environments—preparing for real life.

4

Owner Training Sessions

Two private sessions where you and your family learn all commands, proper corrections, and how to maintain your dog's training long-term.

5

Graduation Day

Your dog demonstrates the “Rule on Proving a Dog in Real-World Situations” with 5 major distractions:

  • Children playing
  • Other dogs nearby
  • People walking dogs
  • Barking dogs
  • Moving vehicles
