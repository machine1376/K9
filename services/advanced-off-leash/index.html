Advanced Training Process

1

Foundation Reinforcement

We ensure your dog has mastered basic obedience before advancing. This includes perfecting sit, stay, down, come, and heel commands in controlled environments.

2

Advanced Command Introduction

Your dog learns new advanced commands using positive reinforcement and appropriate corrections. Each command is taught systematically with clear expectations and consistent reinforcement.

3

Distance Training

Commands are practiced at increasing distances, starting with 10 feet and progressing to 100+ yards. Your dog learns to respond reliably regardless of distance from the handler.

4

Extreme Distraction Training

Your dog practices commands in the most challenging environments: busy parks, around other dogs, near food, with children playing, and during high-energy activities.

5

Owner Integration

Three private sessions where you learn to maintain and reinforce your dog's advanced training. You'll master the timing, corrections, and reinforcement techniques needed for long-term success.

6

Mastery Demonstration

Your dog demonstrates advanced off-leash control with 7 major distractions at 50+ yards:

  • Other dogs off-leash
  • Food on the ground
  • Children playing
  • Moving vehicles
  • Strangers approaching
  • Loud noises
  • Squirrels and wildlife
