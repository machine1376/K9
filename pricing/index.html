Pricing FAQ

What's included in the training cost?

All training programs include board & train services, daily training sessions, professional care and feeding, owner education sessions, and lifetime support. There are no hidden fees or additional charges.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we understand that training is an investment. We offer flexible payment plans to make professional training accessible. Contact us to discuss payment options that work for your budget.

What if my dog needs additional training?

We provide lifetime support for all our graduates. If your dog needs refresher training or additional work, we offer follow-up sessions at a reduced rate for our alumni.

How do I get the rescue dog discount?

Simply mention that your dog is a rescue when you contact us. We'll apply the 15% discount to any training program. We also have trained rescue dogs available for adoption.

What's the difference between on-leash and off-leash training?

On-leash training provides solid basic obedience with leash control. Off-leash training includes everything from on-leash training plus e-collar conditioning and complete off-leash control for ultimate freedom.
