Why Choose K-9 Manners of Texas?

Experience

A History of Success

K-9 Manners of Texas provides personalized obedience and retrieval training, on-leash and off leash. With over 30 years experience, Mike Jeter has trained for local long-standing, reputable kennels and has received stellar recommendations from them, as well as from large private clientele. As an independent contractor, Mike is able to train your dog while providing boarding service at an upscale local kennel where you are assured of its loving and professional care.

Companionship

a family fit

Mike keenly understands that his customers are pet lovers, not just pet owners- as a "member of the family" your dog's good "K-9 manners" are a necessity! Your family's enjoyment of your beloved pet, it's quality of life, and even it's safety are enhanced by its obedience. Give Mike a call to talk about your puppy or adult dog and what you would like to accomplish in its training. A dog trained properly makes all the difference when guiding a pet in the right direction.

Personalized Training

Custom Solutions

Not cookie-cutter classes. Each dog receives customized training for their unique needs. Mike tailors each training program to the individual dog's temperament, behavior, and lifestyle. Whether you're looking for basic obedience, advanced off-leash control, or specialized retrieval training, Mike will work with you to create a training plan that is right for your dog.

Board & Training Programs

One Stop Shop

Professional training and boarding with daily sessions and expert care. Mike will work with you to create a training plan that is right for your dog. If you're not sure if your dog is ready for training, give Mike a call to talk about your puppy or adult dog and what you would like to accomplish in its training.
